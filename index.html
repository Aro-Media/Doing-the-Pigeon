Hosted every year in Peoria by local organization Agilao since 1973, the pigeon convention
is a chance for pigeon lovers around the world to connect with fellow enthusiasts.

We are dedicated to keeping this tradition alive by working with a number of sponsors, ensuring each convention
is bigger than the last!

Our Sponsors include:

  • DIA Pie Manufacturing Co.
  • Walce Flower Foundation
  • Broadcasting National News
  • Bird Sanctuaries United
  • Some random pharmaceutical company literally no one knows about
  • Company from Foreign Country
  • And nearly 50 more!
