Welcome to the official Pigeon Convention website!

Hosted every year in Peoria by local organization Agilao since 1973, the pigeon convention
is a chance for pigeon lovers around the world to connect with fellow enthusiasts.

We are dedicated to keeping this tradition alive by working with a number of sponsors, ensuring each convention
is bigger than the last!

Our Sponsors include:

  • DIA Pie Manufacturing Co.
  • Walce Flower Foundation
  • Broadcasting National News
  • Bird Sanctuaries United
  • Some random pharmaceutical company literally no one knows about
  • Company from Foreign Country
  • And nearly 50 more!

Here at Agilao, Our values are simple.

Our mission is to bring awareness to underappreciated animals. Yep, that's it. For example, everyone loves Ravens.
They're considered cool, and smart. Pigeons, however, get a much worse reputation - despite being just as smart.

We at Agilao wanted to give Pigeons a bit of a spotlight, and so in 1971, our founder Marth Na-Clo made the first
at the Pigeon Convention. Although he only managed to get 1 person to come, that one person was Roy Cal-Oxi,
Agilao's Co-founder. Together, they took '72 off to make some big changes, and by 1973, we had the pigeon
convention as you know today!
